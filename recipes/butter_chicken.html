Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Simple and tasty butter chicken recipe. Written for me as a first test of ChatGPT

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin /li>
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, black pepper, salt, and grated ginger. Add the chicken pieces and stir until coated. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt 2 tablespoons of the butter. Add the onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a separate small saucepan, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter over low heat. Add the heavy cream and garam masala and stir to combine.
  6. Add the cream mixture to the skillet with the chicken and tomato sauce. Stir well to combine and let simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Serve the butter chicken over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
